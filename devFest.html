Welcome to DevFest 2024!

Who are We

The Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) at the University of South Florida is one of the largest and most dynamic student organizations on campus. We are part of a global community supported by Google, dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in the tech world. Our mission is to empower the next generation of tech leaders by providing hands-on learning experiences, fostering innovation, and building a strong, supportive community of like-minded individuals.

About DevFest

Our conferences feature industry experts and thought leaders, technical workshops, a career fair attracting up to 300 students, providing valuable knowledge and fostering community connections.

Our Tracks

Expert Track

Join our Expert Track to share your specialized knowledge on advanced tools, frameworks, AI, software engineering, data science, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies. Inspire students with your insights and help them navigate the dynamic tech landscape.

Entrepreneurship Track

Speak in our Entrepreneurship Track to share your journey to high-level tech positions. Offer guidance on crucial skills and qualities, discuss future tech trends, and provide hiring insights. Inspire the next generation of tech innovators with your expertise.

Spotlight Track

Emerging professionals, share your early career experiences in our Spotlight Track. Provide practical advice on job searching, interview tips, resume building, and overcoming early career challenges. Help guide and inspire those at the start of their professional journey.
