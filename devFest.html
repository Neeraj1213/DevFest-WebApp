Who are We

The Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) at the University of South Florida is one of the largest and most dynamic student organizations on campus. We are part of a global community supported by Google, dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in the tech world. Our mission is to empower the next generation of tech leaders by providing hands-on learning experiences, fostering innovation, and building a strong, supportive community of like-minded individuals.

What is DevFest?

Our conferences feature industry experts and thought leaders, technical workshops, a career fair attracting up to 300 students, providing valuable knowledge and fostering community connections.

Our Tracks

Expert Track

Join our Expert Track to learn from specialists in advanced tools, frameworks, AI, software engineering, data science, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies. Gain valuable insights that will help you navigate the dynamic tech landscape and stay ahead in your field.

Entrepreneurship

Attend our Entrepreneurship Track to hear from successful professionals who have reached high-level tech positions. Learn about the essential skills and qualities for success, discover future tech trends, and gain insider hiring insights. Be inspired by the journeys of tech innovators.

Spotlight Track

Explore our Spotlight Track to hear from professionals sharing their early career experiences. Get practical advice on job searching, interview tips, resume building, and overcoming the challenges of starting your career. Learn from those who were recently in your shoes and gain the tools to kickstart your own journey.
